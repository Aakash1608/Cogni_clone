STUDENT PARTNERSHIP PROGRAMME

In SPP our student partners work as an extension of team cognizance, help in spreading the word about fest and expand our outreach. They are rewarded and motivated to strive to improve their personal skills continuously.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is there any interview/ selection process for the Campus Ambassador program?

Yes there will be a brief interview for shortlisting.

When is this program going to start?

This program will start from mid - December 2021 and continue till end of March 2022.

Can there be multiple SPPs from one college?

Yes, there can be multiple SPPs from a single college.

How much time and effort do I need to devote to this internship?

You need to devote around 2-3 hours every week for this internship.
